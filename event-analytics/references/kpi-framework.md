## KPIs by Event Objective

### Lead Generation (trade shows, conferences)

| KPI | Measurement | Data Source | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|---|
| Qualified leads generated | Count of leads meeting qualification criteria | Badge scans, forms, CRM | 0.3-1.0 per attendee |
| Cost per lead | Total event cost ÷ qualified leads | Budget + CRM | 20-100% of per-attendee cost |
| Lead-to-meeting conversion | Meetings booked ÷ leads | CRM | 15-30% |
| Pipeline generated | Total pipeline value from event leads (90-180 days) | CRM | 3-5x event cost |
| Revenue attributed | Closed deals from event leads (180 days) | CRM | 0.5-1.5x event cost |

**Lead Qualification Criteria:** Define MQL thresholds before the event: e.g., attended live + engaged (poll/Q&A), watched >50% of replay, clicked post-event CTA, or scored by registration data (title seniority, company size). Without agreed criteria, lead targets are unmeasurable.

### Brand Awareness (summits, product launches)

| KPI | Measurement | Data Source | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|---|
| Social mentions | Posts with event hashtag or brand tag | Social listening tool | 0.5-2 per attendee |
| Social reach | Total impressions from event-related posts | Social analytics | 5-20x attendee count |
| Media coverage | Articles, mentions, interviews | Media monitoring | 3-10 pieces for mid-size events |
| Website traffic | Visitors during event week vs. baseline | Web analytics | 50-200% increase |
| Brand sentiment | Positive vs. negative mentions | Social listening | 80%+ positive |

### Customer Engagement (user conferences, partner events)

| KPI | Measurement | Data Source | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|---|
| Session attendance rate | Attendees per session ÷ total attendees | Room scans, app data | 30-60% per session |
| Engagement score | Polls + Q&A + app interactions per attendee | Event platform | 3-5 interactions/person |
| Repeat attendance | % who attended previous edition | Registration data | 30-50% |
| NPS score | Post-event NPS survey | Survey tool | 50+ good, 70+ excellent |
| Customer retention impact | Retention rate of attendees vs. non-attendees (6 months) | CRM | 5-15% higher retention |

### Education / Knowledge Sharing (workshops, training events)

| KPI | Measurement | Data Source | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|---|
| Session ratings | Average rating per session (1-5 scale) | Survey or app | 4.0+ |
| Content downloads | Slide decks and resources downloaded | Event platform | 40-60% of attendees |
| Knowledge gain | Pre/post assessment score change | Survey tool | 15-30% improvement |
| Certificate completion | Attendees completing all required sessions | Tracking system | 70-85% |
| Content NPS | "How useful was the content?" (0-10) | Survey | 7+ average |

## Industry Benchmarks

| Metric | Average | Good | Excellent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Registration-to-attendance rate (paid) | 80-85% | 85-90% | 90%+ |
| Registration-to-attendance rate (free) | 50-60% | 60-70% | 70%+ |
| NPS score | 30-40 | 50-60 | 70+ |
| Session attendance rate | 30-40% | 40-60% | 60%+ |
| Survey response rate | 15-20% | 20-35% | 35%+ |
| Social mentions per attendee | 0.3-0.5 | 0.5-1.0 | 1.0+ |
| Cost per lead (% of per-attendee cost) | 40-100% | 20-40% | Under 20% |

**Virtual CPL note:** For virtual events with low per-attendee costs (<50), evaluate CPL as an absolute number rather than percentage. Virtual webinar CPL benchmark: 15-50 per MQL is typical, regardless of per-attendee cost ratio.
| Pipeline ROI | 2-3x | 3-5x | 5x+ |
| Post-event follow-up rate | 18% (industry avg) | 50-70% | 80%+ |

## Measurement Methods

| Data Point | How to Collect | When |
|---|---|---|
| Attendance | Badge scan at entry, room scans per session | During event |
| Session ratings | In-app rating, post-session quick poll | End of each session |
| NPS | Post-event survey question 1 | T+1 to T+3 days |
| Leads | Badge/QR scans at booths, meeting requests | During + post event |
| Pipeline | CRM tracking with event source tag | T+30 to T+180 days |
| Social metrics | Hashtag tracking, social listening tool | During + T+7 days |
| Content engagement | Platform analytics (views, downloads) | T+1 to T+30 days |
| Financial | Invoice collection, budget reconciliation | T+2 to T+4 weeks |

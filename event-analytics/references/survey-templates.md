## Survey Design Principles

- **Maximum 10 questions** (5-7 ideal). Every additional question reduces completion by 5-10%.
- **Send within 48 hours.** Response rates drop 50% after 72 hours.
- **Start with NPS** — it's the single most benchmarkable metric.
- **Mix closed and open questions.** 70% rating scales, 30% open text.
- **Keep it under 3 minutes.** State this in the survey intro.
- **One incentive works.** "Complete for a chance to win {{incentive}}" boosts response 15-25%.
- **Mobile-optimized.** 50%+ will complete on phone.

## Standard Conference Survey

**Intro text:** "Thank you for attending {{event_name}}! This 3-minute survey helps us make next year even better. Your responses are {{anonymous/confidential}}."

| # | Question | Type | Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | How likely are you to recommend {{event_name}} to a colleague? | NPS (0-10) | Yes |
| 2 | How would you rate your overall experience? | 5-star | Yes |
| 3 | How would you rate the content quality? | 5-star | Yes |
| 4 | How would you rate the networking opportunities? | 5-star | Yes |
| 5 | How would you rate the venue and logistics? | 5-star | Yes |
| 6 | Which session was most valuable to you? | Dropdown (session list) | Yes |
| 7 | What was the most valuable part of the event? | Open text | No |
| 8 | What would you improve for next year? | Open text | No |
| 9 | Will you attend {{event_name}} next year? | Yes / Maybe / No | Yes |
| 10 | Any other comments? | Open text | No |

## Trade Show Survey

Replace session-specific questions with exhibition-focused ones:

| # | Question | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | NPS (0-10) | Scale |
| 2 | Overall experience | 5-star |
| 3 | How useful were the exhibitor booths? | 5-star |
| 4 | Did you find products/services relevant to your needs? | Yes / Somewhat / No |
| 5 | How many exhibitors did you engage with? | Number range |
| 6 | Quality of keynote/session content | 5-star |
| 7 | Most valuable aspect | Open text |
| 8 | What to improve | Open text |
| 9 | Will you attend next year? | Yes / Maybe / No |

## Virtual Event Survey

Add platform-specific questions:

| # | Question | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | NPS (0-10) | Scale |
| 2 | Overall experience | 5-star |
| 3 | How easy was the platform to use? | 5-star |
| 4 | Did you experience any technical issues? | Yes / No + describe |
| 5 | Content quality | 5-star |
| 6 | Engagement features (polls, Q&A, networking) | 5-star |
| 7 | For future events, do you prefer: in-person / virtual / hybrid? | Select |
| 8 | Most valuable session | Dropdown |
| 9 | What to improve | Open text |

## Speaker Feedback Survey

Send to speakers after the event for their experience assessment:

| # | Question | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | How would you rate the overall speaker experience? | 5-star |
| 2 | How well were you briefed before the event? | 5-star |
| 3 | How was the AV/tech support? | 5-star |
| 4 | How was the audience engagement during your session? | 5-star |
| 5 | Would you speak at {{event_name}} again? | Yes / Maybe / No |
| 6 | What could we improve for speakers? | Open text |

## Sponsor Satisfaction Survey

Send to sponsors within 2 weeks post-event:

| # | Question | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | How satisfied are you with your sponsorship experience? (NPS 0-10) | Scale |
| 2 | Were all deliverables from your package fulfilled? | Yes / Partially / No |
| 3 | Quality of leads generated | 5-star |
| 4 | Audience relevance to your business | 5-star |
| 5 | Communication and support from event team | 5-star |
| 6 | Would you sponsor {{event_name}} again? | Yes / Maybe / No |
| 7 | What would increase the value of sponsorship? | Open text |

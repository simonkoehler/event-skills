## Tier Pricing Model

Tier pricing is based on **percentage of total event budget** rather than absolute figures. This scales correctly across markets and currencies.

| Tier | % of Total Event Budget | Max Sponsors | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Platinum / Title | 15-25% | 1-2 | Exclusivity premium — scarcity drives value |
| Gold | 8-15% | 3-5 | Core sponsor tier — highest volume of revenue |
| Silver | 3-8% | 5-10 | Entry-level for larger companies |
| Bronze / Community | 1-3% | Unlimited | Low barrier, high volume |

**Per-attendee pricing alternative:** If the user prefers per-attendee pricing, multiply the per-attendee cost (calibrated via the event-budget skill) by these factors:

| Tier | Per-Attendee Factor | Example (300 attendees, budget anchor = 100/attendee) |
|---|---|---|
| Platinum | 8-15× per-attendee cost | 8-15 × 100 × 300 ÷ 300 = 2,400-4,500 per sponsor |
| Gold | 4-8× | 1,200-2,400 per sponsor |
| Silver | 2-4× | 600-1,200 per sponsor |
| Bronze | 0.5-1.5× | 150-450 per sponsor |

**Pricing principles:**
- Platinum should be 2-3× Gold price (exclusivity premium)
- Each tier should have a clear, tangible value gap to the one above
- Limit Platinum to 1-2 sponsors (scarcity drives value)
- Total sponsorship revenue target: typically 30-50% of event budget for sponsor-funded events
- Ask the user for one comparable event's sponsorship pricing in their market to calibrate absolute numbers

## Exhibitor Specifications

### Standard Booth Types

| Type | Size | Includes | Price Ratio (Standard = 1.0×) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Table top | 2m × 1m table | Table, 2 chairs, 1 power outlet | 0.25-0.4× |
| Standard booth | 3m × 3m (9m²) | Back wall, side walls, table, chairs, lighting, power | 1.0× (baseline) |
| Premium booth | 4m × 3m (12m²) | Same as standard + corner position, extra power | 1.5-2.0× |
| Island booth | 4m × 4m (16m²) | Open all sides, premium location | 2.5-4.0× |
| Custom build | Variable | Shell space only — exhibitor brings own build | Priced per m² by venue |

### Standard Inclusions

- Shell scheme (walls and fascia with company name)
- 1 power outlet (additional at extra cost)
- 1 spotlight
- 1 table + 2 chairs
- WiFi access
- 2 exhibitor badges (additional available for purchase)
- Listing in event directory

### Common Add-Ons (exhibitor pays)

| Item | Price Range (% of table-top booth) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Additional power outlet | 5-15% | Per outlet |
| Dedicated internet line | 15-40% | Hardwired connection |
| Lead scanning device | 15-40% | Per device |
| Furniture rental (extra) | 5-15% per item | Tables, chairs, display units |
| Carpet | Venue-dependent | Standard or premium; priced per m² |
| Extra exhibitor badges | 5-10% each | Often included in packages |

## Sponsor Outreach Timeline

| Timing | Action | Target |
|---|---|---|
| T-10 to T-9 months | Finalize packages and prospectus | — |
| T-9 months | Outreach to returning sponsors (loyalty discount) | Tier 1 |
| T-8 months | Outreach to new Platinum/Gold targets | Tier 1-2 |
| T-7 months | Follow-up on outstanding proposals | All |
| T-6 months | Open Silver/Bronze tiers, broader outreach | Tier 3-4 |
| T-5 months | Close Platinum/Gold sales (or adjust pricing) | Tier 1-2 |
| T-4 months | Sponsor onboarding: collect logos, bios, assets | All confirmed |
| T-3 months | Fulfillment check-in: all deliverables on track? | All confirmed |
| T-2 months | Final fulfillment deadline: logos, content, booth specs | All confirmed |
| T-1 month | Pre-event logistics briefing | All confirmed |
| T+2 weeks | Post-event sponsor report | All |
| T+4 weeks | Renewal conversation for next edition | All |

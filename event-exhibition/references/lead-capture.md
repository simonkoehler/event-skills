## Lead Qualification Framework

Qualify during the conversation, not after. The person scanning the badge adds a tier immediately.

| Tier | Criteria | Follow-Up | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| A — Hot | Decision-maker or strong influencer. Active need or project. Budget confirmed or likely. Requested follow-up. | Personal email from booth contact. Schedule call/meeting. | Within 24 hours |
| B — Warm | Right role, relevant company. Interest expressed but no active project. "Come back to us in Q3." | Personalized template email. Add to nurture with priority. | Within 48 hours |
| C — Cool | Relevant industry but no buying signal. Collected info. Took swag. | Automated nurture sequence. No personal follow-up. | Within 1 week |
| D — Disqualify | Student, competitor, wrong industry, or just collecting giveaways. | No follow-up. Don't add to CRM pipeline. | Never |

### Mapping to CRM lead stages (MQL / SQL)

Users often state lead goals in HubSpot/Salesforce vocabulary ("250 MQLs"). Translate as follows:

| Skill Tier | CRM equivalent | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| A — Hot | **SQL** (Sales-Qualified Lead) | Booked meeting or active deal context; goes directly to AE / SDR for follow-up. |
| B — Warm | **MQL** (Marketing-Qualified Lead) | Right ICP fit, interest expressed, no immediate timeline; goes to nurture sequence + scoring. |
| C — Cool | **Lead** (top-of-funnel) | Information seeker; marketing automation only. |
| D — Disqualify | Not added to CRM pipeline | Note for competitive intel if relevant. |

**Translating user goals:**
- "We want 250 MQLs from this show" → target is A+B count (some teams) or B-only (most teams). Confirm with the user which definition they use.
- "We need 50 SQLs" → target is A-only count.
- "100 leads" → ambiguous; clarify which tier(s) count before sizing booth and staff.

**Qualification questions (ask naturally, not as interrogation):**
- "What brings you to the show?" (intent signal)
- "What's your current setup for [relevant topic]?" (need signal)
- "Are you evaluating solutions right now, or more exploring?" (timing signal)
- "Who else is involved in this decision?" (buying committee signal)

## Lead Capture Tools

| Tool | How It Works | Best For | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| Organizer badge scanner | App provided by event. Scans QR/barcode on badge. | Standard — use when available. | Usually included in booth package |
| Own scanning app | Akkroo, Momencio, iCapture, Leadature | Custom qualification fields, CRM integration | 1-5% of booth budget per event |
| Business cards | Paper. Manual entry. | Backup only. Cards get lost and misread. | Free (but costly to process) |
| Paper lead form | Printed A5 form: name, company, email, notes, tier | Emergency backup when tech fails | Negligible |
| LinkedIn QR connect | Scan personal QR codes in LinkedIn app | 1:1 executive conversations | Free |

**Always have a paper backup.** WiFi goes down at trade shows. Apps crash. Battery dies. A stack of simple paper forms saves leads when technology fails.

## Follow-Up Email Templates

### A-Lead: Personal, Same-Day

```
Subject: Great meeting you at {{event_name}} — next steps

Hi {{first_name}},

Good talking with you today at our booth. {{One specific reference to
what you discussed — proves it's not a template.}}

As mentioned, I'd suggest {{specific next step: a call to dive deeper /
a demo focused on [their use case] / connecting you with our [specialist]}}.

Would {{proposed_date}} work for a 30-minute call?

{{sender_name}}
{{sender_title}}
```

### B-Lead: Personalized Template, 48h

```
Subject: Following up from {{event_name}}

Hi {{first_name}},

We spoke briefly at {{event_name}} about {{topic_discussed}}.

{{One relevant resource: case study, whitepaper, or product page that 
matches their interest — not a generic "learn more" link.}}

When you're ready to explore this further, I'm happy to set up a
conversation. No rush — just wanted to make sure you had this.

{{sender_name}}
```

### C-Lead: Automated Nurture

```
Subject: Resources from {{event_name}}

Hi {{first_name}},

Thanks for stopping by our booth at {{event_name}}.

Here are some resources you might find useful:
- [Resource 1 relevant to the event's theme]
- [Resource 2]

We'll keep you updated on [relevant content] — unsubscribe anytime.

The {{company_name}} team
```

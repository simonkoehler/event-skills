## Asset Matrix Per Session

One 45-minute conference session can yield 20+ content pieces:

| Asset Type | Quantity | Production Effort | Channel | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Full recording (on-demand) | 1 | Low (upload + light edit) | Event platform, YouTube | Week 1 |
| Summary blog post (500-800 words) | 1 | Medium (write from transcript) | Company blog | Week 1-2 |
| Short-form video clips (30-90 sec) | 3-5 | Medium (edit from recording) | LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram Reels | Week 2-3 |
| Quote graphics | 5-10 | Low (design from transcript) | LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram | Week 1-4 |
| Slide deck PDF download | 1 | Low (format + upload) | Event website, email | Week 1 |
| Deep-dive article (1,000+ words) | 1-3 | High (original writing) | Blog, guest posts | Week 3-6 |
| Podcast episode | 1 | Medium (audio extract or interview) | Podcast platforms | Week 2-4 |
| LinkedIn carousel | 1 | Medium (design key takeaways) | LinkedIn | Week 2-3 |
| Newsletter segment | 1 | Low (excerpt + link) | Email newsletter | Week 1-2 |
| Infographic | 1 | High (design from data/frameworks) | Blog, social, downloads | Week 4-8 |
| FAQ post (from Q&A) | 1 | Low (transcribe + organize) | Blog, knowledge base | Week 2-3 |

**Multiplier for full events:** A 2-day conference with 30 sessions can yield 80-100+ content pieces over 6 months, compared to 10-15 with traditional (non-AI) methods.

## Repurposing Pipeline

### Phase 1: Momentum (Week 1 — T+0 to T+7 days)

**Goal:** Capitalize on post-event energy and social media buzz.

| Day | Action | Asset |
|---|---|---|
| T+1 | Send thank-you email with photo highlights | Email |
| T+1-2 | Publish recap post with "by the numbers" stats | LinkedIn, Twitter/X |
| T+2 | Send survey email | Email |
| T+3-5 | Publish 3-5 quote graphics from keynotes | LinkedIn, Instagram |
| T+5-7 | Publish keynote recap blog post (500-800 words per keynote) | Blog |
| T+7 | Send content follow-up email with first recordings | Email |

### Phase 2: Depth (Weeks 2-4 — T+8 to T+28 days)

**Goal:** Extend event reach to non-attendees through high-value content.

| Week | Action | Asset |
|---|---|---|
| Week 2 | Publish all session recordings on-demand | Event platform |
| Week 2 | Release slide decks as downloadable PDFs | Event website |
| Week 2-3 | Cut 3-5 short-form video clips per keynote | LinkedIn, social |
| Week 2-3 | Publish LinkedIn carousels with session takeaways | LinkedIn |
| Week 3 | Write 2-3 deep-dive blog posts from top sessions | Blog |
| Week 3 | Create podcast episode from best panel discussion | Podcast |
| Week 4 | Compile FAQ post from audience Q&A across sessions | Blog |
| Week 4 | Send sponsor fulfillment reports | Email |

### Phase 3: Themes (Months 2-3 — T+5 to T+12 weeks)

**Goal:** Transform event content into themed campaigns and gated assets.

| Week | Action | Asset |
|---|---|---|
| Month 2 | Create themed content series (e.g., "5 Insights from {{event_name}}") | Blog, social |
| Month 2 | Produce 1-2 infographics from data-heavy sessions | Blog, social, downloads |
| Month 2 | Write white paper or report synthesizing multiple sessions | Gated download |
| Month 3 | Host follow-up webinar with top-rated speakers | Webinar platform |
| Month 3 | Develop case studies from attendee success stories | Sales enablement |

### Phase 4: Evergreen (Months 4-6+ — T+13 to T+26 weeks)

**Goal:** Build long-term SEO value and maintain community engagement.

| Month | Action | Asset |
|---|---|---|
| Month 4 | Compile "greatest hits" content collection | Resource hub |
| Month 4-5 | Convert workshop materials into training content | LMS, knowledge base |
| Month 5 | Publish year-in-review or trend analysis from event themes | Blog, report |
| Month 6 | Launch next-edition promotional campaign using best content | Email, social |
| Month 6 | Repurpose top-performing assets as ads or lead magnets | Paid campaigns |

## Content Calendar Template

Generate a content calendar table with these columns:

| Publish Date | Asset Type | Source Session | Title/Description | Channel | Status | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| {{date}} | Blog post | Keynote by {{speaker_name}} | "3 Takeaways from..." | Blog | Draft | {{owner}} |
| {{date}} | Video clip | Panel: {{session_title}} | 60-sec highlight | LinkedIn | Pending edit | {{owner}} |
| {{date}} | Quote graphic | {{speaker_name}} keynote | "{{quote_excerpt}}" | LinkedIn, Instagram | Ready | {{owner}} |

**Scheduling rules:**
- Week 1: 1 piece per day (riding the momentum wave)
- Weeks 2-4: 3-4 pieces per week
- Months 2-3: 2-3 pieces per week
- Months 4-6: 1-2 pieces per week
- Never publish more than 2 pieces from the same session in one week (variety matters)

## Priority Ranking

When deciding which sessions to repurpose first:

| Priority | Criteria | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Keynote sessions | Highest visibility, broadest appeal, best brand association |
| 2 | Highest-rated sessions (survey data) | Proven audience value |
| 3 | Sessions with external speakers (not internal) | Third-party credibility |
| 4 | Sessions on trending topics | SEO and social potential |
| 5 | Workshop/hands-on sessions | Evergreen training value |
| 6 | Panel discussions | Multiple perspectives, good for podcast format |
| 7 | Lightning talks | Quick, shareable, high volume |

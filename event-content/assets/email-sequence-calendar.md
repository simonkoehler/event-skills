# Email Sequence Calendar

> Visual email sequence with timing recommendations. Populate with actual dates calculated from the event date.

## Standard 12-Month Sequence

For events with 6+ months lead time. For shorter lead times, see the compressed sequence below.

| # | Email Type | Timing | To | Subject Line Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Save-the-date | T-8 to T-12 months | Full list | "Mark Your Calendar: {{event_name}} — {{event_date_display}}" |
| 2 | Official invitation | T-6 to T-8 weeks | Full list | "You're Invited: {{event_name}} in {{venue_city}}" |
| 3 | Early-bird | With invite; deadline T-3 to T-5 months | Non-registered | "Save {{currency_symbol}}{{discount_amount}} — Early bird ends {{early_bird_deadline}}" |
| 4a | Speaker announcement 1 | T-5 months | Full list | "{{speaker_name}} is joining {{event_name}}" |
| 4b | Speaker announcement 2 | T-4 months | Full list | "Just announced: {{speaker_name}} on {{session_topic_short}}" |
| 4c | Speaker announcement 3 | T-3 months | Full list | "{{speaker_name}} completes our lineup" |
| 5 | Agenda release | T-2 to T-3 months | Full list | "The full {{event_name}} agenda is here" |
| 6 | Last-chance registration | T-1 to T-2 weeks | Non-registered | "Only {{seats_remaining}} seats left" |
| 7 | Logistics / KBYG | T-2 to T-4 weeks | Registered only | "Your {{event_name}} prep guide" |
| 8 | 1-week reminder | T-7 days | Registered only | "{{event_name}} is one week away" |
| 9 | 1-day reminder | T-1 day | Registered only | "See you tomorrow at {{event_name}}" |
| 10 | Thank-you | T+1 day | Attendees + no-shows (segmented) | "Thank you for making {{event_name}} unforgettable" |
| 11 | Survey | T+1 to T+3 days | Attendees | "3 minutes to shape next year's {{event_name}}" |
| 12 | Content follow-up | T+1 to T+2 weeks | All registered | "{{event_name}} recordings and slides are live" |
| 13 | Next-edition teaser | T+2 to T+4 weeks | All registered | "{{event_name}} {{next_year}} — save the date" |

## Compressed Sequence (<3 months lead time)

For events with short lead times. Drop save-the-date and rolling speaker announcements.

| # | Email Type | Timing | To |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Invitation (combines invite + early-bird) | Immediately | Full list |
| 2 | Speaker/agenda combo | T-4 to T-6 weeks | Full list |
| 3 | Last-chance registration | T-1 week | Non-registered |
| 4 | Logistics / KBYG | T-1 to T-2 weeks | Registered only |
| 5 | 1-day reminder | T-1 day | Registered only |
| 6 | Thank-you | T+1 day | Attendees + no-shows |
| 7 | Survey | T+2 days | Attendees |
| 8 | Content follow-up | T+1 week | All registered |

## Micro Event Sequence (VIP dinner, 10-50 people)

Replace mass emails with personalized communications:

| # | Email Type | Timing | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Personalized invitation | T-3 to T-4 weeks | Individual email, not template blast |
| 2 | RSVP follow-up | T-2 weeks | Personal follow-up to non-responders |
| 3 | Logistics note | T-3 to T-5 days | Venue, dress code, parking, dietary question |
| 4 | Thank-you note | T+1 day | Personalized, reference specific conversation |
| 5 | Follow-up on commitments | T+1 week | If action items were discussed |

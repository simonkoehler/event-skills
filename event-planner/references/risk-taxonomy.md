# Event Risk Taxonomy

Structured risk categories aligned with the MBECS Risk Management competency domain.

Select 8-12 risks most relevant to the user's event type, format, and scale. Each category below includes likelihood context to help calibrate risk scoring.

## 1. Strategic Risks

| Risk | Typical Likelihood | Typical Impact | Common Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Event objectives misaligned with organizational goals | Low | High | Poor stakeholder alignment during concept phase |
| Target audience mismatch (wrong people attend) | Medium | High | Unclear audience definition, broad marketing |
| Competitor event on same date or in same market | Medium | Medium | Insufficient market research, industry calendar overlap |
| Executive sponsor withdraws support | Low | Critical | Leadership change, budget cuts, strategic pivot |
| Scope creep (event grows beyond original brief) | High | Medium | Stakeholder requests, "while we're at it" additions |
| Event positioned poorly relative to market trends | Low | High | Lack of industry awareness, outdated format |

## 2. Financial Risks

| Risk | Typical Likelihood | Typical Impact | Common Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Budget overrun | High | High | Top trigger: AV/production scope creep. Also: late vendor additions, currency fluctuation, underestimated F&B |
| Sponsor withdrawal or late payment | Medium | High | Sponsor budget cuts, competitor conflict, poor fulfillment of previous edition |
| Registration shortfall (<70% of target by T-4 weeks) | Medium | High | Weak value proposition, poor marketing, bad timing, economic conditions |
| Registration significantly exceeds capacity | Low | Medium | Viral promotion, underestimated demand — good problem but creates logistics stress |
| Currency fluctuation (international events) | Medium | Medium | Long lead times between contract signing and payment |
| Vendor price increases between contract and event | Low | Medium | Inflation, supply chain disruptions, late changes to specifications |
| Ticket refund wave (>10% within 2 weeks of event) | Low | High | External crisis, competitor event, negative publicity |

## 3. Operational Risks

| Risk | Typical Likelihood | Typical Impact | Common Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Visa denial or delay for international speakers/attendees | Medium (for non-EU/US events with 20%+ international audience) | High | Long consular processing times (some passport countries: 8-12 weeks), Schengen visa quota constraints, incomplete invitation letters. Mitigation: issue visa invitation letters at T-12 weeks minimum, maintain a country-by-country processing-time list, build buffer into the invitation acceptance pool. Owner: Registration Lead. |
| Venue cancellation or force majeure | Low | Critical | Construction issues, natural disaster, double-booking, pandemic restrictions |
| Key vendor no-show or underperformance | Medium | High | Vendor overcommitment, communication breakdown, financial issues |
| Catering failure (dietary requirements missed, quantity wrong) | Medium | Medium | Incomplete attendee data, vendor miscommunication, last-minute headcount changes |
| Badge/registration system failure at check-in | Medium | High | Untested system, WiFi dependency, power failure |
| Power outage | Low | Critical | Aging infrastructure, weather, construction |
| Internet/WiFi outage | Medium | High | Insufficient bandwidth, ISP issues, venue infrastructure |
| Weather disruption | Medium (varies) | Medium-High | Outdoor events, travel impact, venue access |
| Transportation disruption (flight cancellations, rail strikes) | Medium | Medium | Airline issues, labor action, extreme weather |
| Security incident (unauthorized access) | Low | High | Inadequate credentialing, venue access control gaps |
| Health emergency (attendee medical event) | Low | High | Lack of first aid preparation, venue far from hospital |

## 4. Content & Speaker Risks

| Risk | Typical Likelihood | Typical Impact | Common Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Keynote speaker cancellation within 2 weeks of event | Low | Critical | Illness, travel emergency, scheduling conflict |
| Speaker presentation not submitted by deadline | High | Medium | Speaker overcommitment, unclear deadlines, no enforcement |
| Content quality below expectations | Medium | High | Insufficient vetting, no rehearsal, topic mismatch |
| Panel discussion falls flat | Medium | Medium | No pre-call between panelists, weak moderation, wrong chemistry |
| Demo failure during product launch | Medium | Critical | Insufficient testing, network dependency, hardware failure |
| Speaker no-show (non-keynote) | Medium | Medium | Travel issues, miscommunication, overcommitment |
| CFP yields insufficient quality submissions | Medium | High | Poor promotion, unclear criteria, wrong audience reach |
| Content not adapted for virtual audience (hybrid) | High | Medium | Speakers present to room only, ignore camera |

## 5. Technology Risks

| Risk | Typical Likelihood | Typical Impact | Common Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| WiFi failure under load | Medium | High | Untested at expected capacity, venue infrastructure, interference |
| Streaming platform outage (hybrid/virtual) | Low | Critical | Platform reliability, ISP issues, configuration error |
| Event app crash or slow performance | Medium | Medium | Server capacity, untested with real data, API failures |
| AV equipment failure | Medium | High | No backup equipment, cable failure, incompatible formats |
| Recording failure | Medium | High | Storage full, operator error, format incompatibility |
| Virtual attendee cannot access platform | Medium | Medium | Browser compatibility, firewall restrictions, user error |
| Backup stream not configured | Medium | Critical (if primary fails) | Oversight, cost cutting, assumed reliability |
| Presentation laptop incompatibility | High | Low-Medium | Multiple presenters, different OS, connector issues |

## 6. Compliance & Legal Risks

| Risk | Typical Likelihood | Typical Impact | Common Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| GDPR violation (attendee data mishandled) | Medium | Critical | Sharing data with sponsors without consent, unsecured data export, missing privacy notice |
| Photo/video consent not obtained | High | Medium | No opt-out mechanism, no signage, no consent form |
| Accessibility requirements not met | Medium | High | Venue not ADA/EN-compliant, no captioning, no wheelchair access |
| Visa issues for international speakers | Medium | High | Late application, incorrect documentation, embassy delays |
| Insurance gaps (public liability, cancellation) | Low | Critical | Assumed venue insurance covers all scenarios (it usually doesn't) |
| Alcohol liability (over-serving at reception) | Low | High | Open bar without trained staff, no drink limits |
| Fire code violation (over-capacity room) | Low | High | Room capacity not monitored, popular session overflow |
| Contract dispute with vendor | Low | Medium | Ambiguous scope, verbal agreements, missing cancellation terms |

## 7. Reputational Risks

| Risk | Typical Likelihood | Typical Impact | Common Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Negative social media incident during event | Low | High | Controversial speaker statement, poor attendee experience, visible problem |
| Diversity and inclusion criticism | Medium | High | Non-diverse speaker lineup, inaccessible venue, non-inclusive language |
| Sponsor controversy (sponsor brand conflict) | Low | Medium | Sponsor in news for negative reasons, audience backlash |
| Harassment incident | Low | Critical | No code of conduct, insufficient enforcement, poor reporting mechanism |
| Environmental sustainability criticism | Low | Medium | Excessive waste, single-use plastics, no sustainability plan |

## Risk Selection by Event Type

Use this guide to prioritize which risk categories to include based on event type:

| Event Type | Priority Risk Categories | Always Include |
|---|---|---|
| Trade show / Expo | Operational, Financial, Compliance | Vendor no-show, exhibitor logistics, budget overrun |
| Partner conference | Strategic, Content, Reputational | Audience mismatch, content quality, relationship damage |
| Product launch | Content, Technology, Reputational | Demo failure, messaging leak, media misalignment |
| Sales kickoff | Strategic, Content, Operational | Leadership alignment, training quality, venue logistics |
| Developer conference | Content, Technology, Compliance | CFP quality, WiFi, recording failure, code of conduct |
| Industry summit | Strategic, Content, Reputational | Speaker quality, diversity, thought leadership credibility |
| Roadshow | Operational, Financial, Content | Multi-city logistics, brand consistency, equipment shipping |
| Community meetup | Operational, Financial | Venue booking, speaker cancellation, low turnout |
| Micro event | Strategic, Operational | Key guest cancellation, venue/restaurant issue, dietary miss |
| Virtual event | Technology, Content, Compliance | Platform outage, engagement drop-off, recording failure |
| Hybrid event | Technology, Operational, Content | Streaming failure, dual-audience experience gap, tech complexity |

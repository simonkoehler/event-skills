## Email Marketing

**The highest-ROI channel for event promotion.** Average ROI: $36-42 per $1 spent.

### Sequence (8-12 emails over promotion period)

| # | Email | Audience | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Save-the-date | Full list | T-12 to T-8 months |
| 2 | Registration open + early-bird | Full list | When registration opens |
| 3 | Speaker announcement #1 | Full list | T-5 months |
| 4 | Early-bird deadline reminder | Non-registered | 1 week before early-bird ends |
| 5 | Agenda release | Full list | T-2 to T-3 months |
| 6 | Speaker announcement #2 | Non-registered | T-6 weeks |
| 7 | Social proof ("X people registered") | Non-registered | T-4 weeks |
| 8 | Last chance | Non-registered | T-1 to T-2 weeks |
| 9 | "From the organizer" (plain text, personal) | Non-registered | T-3 days |

### Segmentation Strategy

| Segment | Email Focus | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Not-yet-registered | Registration CTAs, benefits, social proof | Full sequence |
| Already registered | Logistics, agenda, preparation | Informational only |
| Past attendees | "You loved it last year" + loyalty pricing | Priority access |
| Speaker network | Ask to share with their audience | 1-2 asks |
| VIP / executive targets | Personal invitation from leadership | 1-2 personal |

### Performance Benchmarks

| Metric | Average | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Open rate | 36-42% | 45%+ |
| Click-through rate | 2-5% | 5%+ |
| Conversion (click → register) | 5-15% | 10%+ |
| Unsubscribe rate | 0.08-0.5% | Under 0.3% |
| Best send day | Tuesday-Thursday | Test for your audience |
| Best send time | 8:00-11:30 AM | Recipient's timezone |

## LinkedIn

**Primary B2B event promotion channel.** ~80% of B2B social leads come from LinkedIn.

### Organic Strategy

| Content Type | Frequency | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Speaker spotlights | 1-2 per week | Photo + text (link in comments) |
| Session teasers | 1 per week | Carousel or text-only |
| Countdown posts | At milestones (30, 14, 7, 1 day) | Text + graphic |
| Behind-the-scenes | 1 per week | Photo, casual tone |
| Social proof | 1-2 per promotion period | "X professionals registered" |

**Key rules:** Links in first comment (not body — algorithm penalty). First 2 lines must hook (before "see more"). 1-3 hashtags only. Tag speakers and sponsors. Native video (15-60s) gets 3x engagement vs. links. Employee advocacy multiplies reach 5-10x.

### Paid Strategy

| Ad Type | Best For | Budget Range (% of marketing budget) | Relative CPC (baseline = 1.0×) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sponsored Content | Awareness + registration | 15-40% | 1.0× |
| Event Ads | Registration conversion | 10-20% | 1.5-2.0× |
| Lead Gen Forms | Registration without leaving LinkedIn | 15-30% | 2.5-3.0× per lead |
| Retargeting | Converting website visitors | 5-10% | 0.5-0.8× |

**Targeting:** Job title, company size, industry, seniority. Lookalike audiences from past attendee lists. Exclude current registrants.

### LinkedIn Event

Create a LinkedIn Event for discoverability. Invite connections. Post updates to the Event page. Appears in attendees' feeds.

## Paid Advertising

### Google Ads

| Campaign Type | When to Use | Budget (% of marketing budget) | Relative CPA (baseline = 1.0×) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Search (event name + keywords) | Always — captures active intent | 10-20% | 1.0× |
| Search (competitor events) | If budget allows | 5-10% | 1.3-1.5× |
| Display retargeting | Always — recovers website visitors | 5-10% | 0.6-0.8× |

**Keywords:** Event name, "[industry] conference [city] [year]", "[topic] summit", competitor event names.

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)

Lower priority for B2B but useful for broad awareness at lower CPC. Best for: community events, developer events, consumer-facing launches.

## Partner and Speaker Amplification

The most underused and highest-value channel. Speaker and partner networks reach exactly the right audience.

### Speaker Amplification Kit

Provide each confirmed speaker with:
- 3 pre-written social posts (they customize but don't have to write from scratch)
- Event banner graphic sized for LinkedIn cover photo
- Direct registration link with speaker-specific tracking code
- Email blurb they can forward to their network

**Expected impact:** Each speaker sharing reaches 500-5,000 relevant professionals. 10 speakers × 1,000 average network = 10,000 targeted impressions at zero cost.

### Partner Co-Promotion

| Action | What You Provide | What Partner Does |
|---|---|---|
| Email swap | Email copy + registration link | Send to their list |
| Social swap | Pre-written posts + graphics | Share on their channels |
| Blog swap | Guest post for their blog | Publish with link to registration |
| Discount code | Partner-specific promo code | Share with their community |

## Content Marketing and SEO

Lower urgency but builds long-term event discovery.

| Content Type | SEO Value | Effort | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Event landing page | High (primary keyword target) | Medium | Before registration opens |
| Speaker profile pages | Medium (name-based search) | Low | As speakers confirmed |
| Session description pages | Medium (topic-based search) | Low | When agenda published |
| Blog posts on event topics | High (thought leadership + SEO) | Medium | 8-12 weeks before event |
| Past event recap post | Medium (proves track record) | Low | After previous edition |

## Conversion Funnel Benchmarks

| Stage | Metric | Benchmark | Optimize If Below |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ad impression → Click | CTR | 1-3% | Improve targeting or creative |
| Click → Landing page view | Bounce rate | 30-50% bounce | Improve page load speed, messaging match |
| Landing page → Registration page | Navigation rate | 20-40% | Improve value proposition, CTA placement |
| Registration page → Completed | Form conversion | 30-60% (paid events) | Reduce fields, add social proof, improve UX |
| Registered → Attended | Show-up rate | 80-90% (paid) | Better pre-event communication |

**Rule of thumb:** To get 500 registrations with average benchmarks, you need approximately 50,000 targeted impressions (1% CTR × 30% landing page × 33% form = ~0.1% end-to-end conversion). Adjust calculations based on your audience quality and channel mix.

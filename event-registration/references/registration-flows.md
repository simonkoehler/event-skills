## Form Design Principles

- Every additional required field reduces conversions by up to 11%
- Single-page forms convert better than multi-step for events under 500 attendees
- 55%+ of B2B registrations happen on mobile — design mobile-first
- Place CTA button above the fold and repeat at the bottom
- "Save My Seat" outperforms "Register Now" by 10-15%
- Show social proof near submit: "{{registered_count}} professionals already registered"
- Add urgency triggers: countdown timer, "{{seats_remaining}} spots left"
- Single focused CTA increases conversions by 3.7x vs. multiple CTAs

## Field Specifications by Event Type

### Conference (paid, 200+ attendees)
Required: first name, last name, email, company, job title, ticket type, country
Optional: dietary requirements, accessibility needs, session preferences
EU required: marketing consent (separate checkbox), sponsor data sharing consent

### Meetup (free, under 200)
Required: first name, last name, email, company
Optional: dietary requirements (if catering)
Skip: job title, country, session preferences

### Trade show (exhibitors + attendees)
Attendee: first name, last name, email, company, job title, interests/tracks
Exhibitor: company, booth contact, company description, products/services, electrical needs

### Virtual event
Required: first name, last name, email, company, timezone
Optional: session preferences, topics of interest
Skip: dietary, accessibility (physical)

### VIP/invite-only
Required: invitation code, first name, last name, email
Optional: dietary, accessibility, plus-one
Skip: ticket selection, company (already known)

## Conversion Optimization

| Element | Impact | Implementation |
|---|---|---|
| Social proof | +40% near CTA | "Join 2,400 leaders" badge near register button |
| Urgency timer | Up to +332% | Countdown to early-bird deadline or event date |
| Price anchoring | Significant | Display highest tier first, mark middle as "Most Popular" |
| Trust signals | +10-20% | Logos of past sponsors, attendee testimonials, rating |
| Progress indicator | +10-15% | If multi-step, show "Step 2 of 3" |
| Single CTA | +3.7x | One action per page, not competing buttons |
| Mobile optimization | Required | Thumb-friendly buttons, auto-fill support, minimal scrolling |
| Guest checkout | +15-25% | Don't require account creation |

## Waitlist Management

When event reaches capacity:

1. Convert registration button to "Join Waitlist"
2. Collect: name, email, ticket type preference
3. When spot opens: notify first on list, give 48h to confirm
4. If no confirmation in 48h: move to next person
5. Send weekly waitlist status updates ("You are #12 on the waitlist")
6. Close waitlist at T-1 week (no time for logistics for late additions)

**No-show overbooking strategy (free events):**

| Expected Capacity | Register Up To | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| 100 | 140-150 | 30-50% no-show rate for free events |
| 200 | 280-300 | Same ratio |
| 500 | 650-700 | Slightly lower no-show % at larger scale |

**No-show overbooking (paid events):**

| Expected Capacity | Register Up To | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| 100 | 108-112 | 5-15% no-show rate for paid events |
| 500 | 540-560 | Same ratio |
